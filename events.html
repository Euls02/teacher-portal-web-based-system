School Events and Activities

School Events / Celebrations

  • Buwan ng Wika (August – celebration of Filipino language and culture)
  • Nutrition Month (July)
  • United Nations Day (October – cultural presentations of different countries)
  • Teachers' Day (October 5 – honoring teachers)
  • Foundation Day (varies by school – anniversary celebration)
  • Intramurals / Sports Fest (varies – school-wide sports competition)
  • Christmas Party / Christmas Program (December)
  • Valentine's Day Program (February – sometimes celebrated with activities)
  • Moving Up / Recognition Day (end of school year – awarding of honors)
  • Graduation / Commencement Exercises (end of school year – for graduating students)

National Holidays Observed in Schools

  • Independence Day (June 12)
  • National Heroes Day (August)
  • Bonifacio Day (November 30)
  • Rizal Day (December 30)
  • EDSA People Power Anniversary (February 25)
  • Ninoy Aquino Day (August 21)
  • All Saints' Day / All Souls' Day (November 1–2)
